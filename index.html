Bronx Eighth Graders Prepare for High School Admissions

It is never too early to start.

By Diana Escamilla | Nov. 10, 2023

Jordan Vargas, 12, and Destiny Torres, 13, claim that attending the right high school is crucial to secure a successful future.

Every day after regular classes are over, these two Bronx eighth grade students at the Rafael Hernandez Dual Language Magnet School stay until 5 p.m. cultivating their passions and ensuring academic excellence.

WHEDco, a nonprofit organization, “educates 8th graders and families about their high school options, helps them build lists of schools based on needs or interest, provides application support, takes them on school tours, and provides admissions test prep through a partnership with BeSpoke” said WHEDco’s Vice President Zac Roy.

Tensions are high in the month of November as testing is currently undergoing and students prepare for auditions and interviews, and finalize their essays before the deadline on Dec. 1.
